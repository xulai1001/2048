How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the Snake and eat the tiles . When you eat a tile with the same number to your head, they merge into one!

Hint: You can eat yourself to get bigger tiles.

Hint: You will not die if you crash into walls or yourself. You can always turn away.

Hint: You can also win if you get all the Magic words! Happy April Fool's Day!
